### Regular Meetings

**General Meetings** are held on the **2nd Tuesday of every month**, September through June, at **10:00 AM** in **Templars Hall, Old Poway Park**.

All members and prospective members are welcome to attend.

---

### Annual Events

- **"Celebrate Women" Art Exhibit** — showcasing women artists in the community
- **Student Art Exhibit** — featuring work from local student artists
- **Theatre in the Park** — live community performances at Old Poway Park
- **Old-Fashioned Friendship Tea** — an afternoon tea with proceeds supporting the club's charitable causes
